GeoConv

Convert sheetdata1 geolocation, distance and angle into a new geolocation.

1) First sheet should contain named columns in the first row; latitude, longitude, distance, angle. The program ignores capitalization.

Drop file here or click choose a file..

Formula: φ2 = asin( sin φ1 ⋅ cos δ + cos φ1 ⋅ sin δ ⋅ cos θ )
λ2 = λ1 + atan2( sin θ ⋅ sin δ ⋅ cos φ1, cos δ − sin φ1 ⋅ sin φ2 )
where φ is latitude, λ is longitude, θ is the bearing (clockwise from north), δ is the angular distance d/R; d being the distance travelled, R the earth’s radius
